My Personal Development

I would say most of my personal development took place in highschool. During my time at Ardenne I was also a member of the cricket team. I spent most of my days after school on the field playing cricket. This taught me hardwork, discipline and teamwork. These are 3 of my main strengths that I have developed and continue to develop over the years. Being a part of a sporting team has taught me to work with people from different types of backgrounds a long as they are willing to commit to a task and get work done.
