Photography Is My Passion

“Photography is the story I fail to put into words.”— Destin Sparks

I have been intrested in photography for as long as I can remember, for be it is both a hobby and a passion. When I got my first phone with a camera I used to take photos of randoms stuff around my yard. I did the same when i got my first camera in grade 7, I still have that camera. It was a kodak easyshare c182. Nowadays I mostly lean towards cannon cameras preferrably the cannon eos rebel t6. However the camera doesn't matter because I can use my iphone to get some very decent shots. My favourite type of shoots are fashion shoots and nature shots. I also really like shooting and editing videos. My other intrests include playing video games and watching anime. I enjoy playing games such as call of duty and fortnite.
